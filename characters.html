Straw Hat Crew

The Straw Hat Pirates, captained by Monkey D. Luffy, are a tight-knit crew of 10 core members. Each member boasts a specific role—such as navigator, doctor, and cook—and a personal dream they are chasing. They sail on their flagship, the Thousand Sunny, recognized as an Emperor-level crew.
